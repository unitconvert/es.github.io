How to Convert Dekajoule/second to Horsepower (water)

1 daJ/s = 0.0134040531 horsepower (water)
1 horsepower (water) = 74.6043 daJ/s

Example: convert 15 daJ/s to horsepower (water):
15 daJ/s = 15 × 0.0134040531 horsepower (water) = 0.2010607968 horsepower (water)
