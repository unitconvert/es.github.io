How to Convert Centijoule/second to Foot Pound-force/second

1 foot pound-force/second = 135.5817948329 cJ/s

Example: convert 15 cJ/s to foot pound-force/second:
15 cJ/s = 15 × 0.0073756215 foot pound-force/second = 0.1106343224 foot pound-force/second
