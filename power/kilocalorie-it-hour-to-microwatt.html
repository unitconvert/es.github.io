Cómo convertir Kilocaloría (IT)/hora a Microvatio

1 kcal/h = 1163000 µW
1 µW = 8,5984522785898E-7 kcal/h

Example: convert 15 kcal/h to µW:
15 kcal/h = 15 × 1163000 µW = 17445000 µW
