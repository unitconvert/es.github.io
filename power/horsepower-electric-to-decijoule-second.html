How to Convert Horsepower (electric) to Decijoule/second

1 horsepower (electric) = 7460 dJ/s
1 dJ/s = 0.0001340483 horsepower (electric)

Example: convert 15 horsepower (electric) to dJ/s:
15 horsepower (electric) = 15 × 7460 dJ/s = 111900 dJ/s
