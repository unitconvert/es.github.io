How to Convert Newton/square Centimeter to Millibar

1 newton/square centimeter = 100 mbar
1 mbar = 0.01 newton/square centimeter

Example: convert 15 newton/square centimeter to mbar:
15 newton/square centimeter = 15 × 100 mbar = 1500 mbar
