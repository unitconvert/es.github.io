How to Convert Cubic Centimeter/minute to Cubic Foot/hour

1 cubic centimeter/minute = 0.00211888 ft^3/h
1 ft^3/h = 471.9474432 cubic centimeter/minute

Example: convert 15 cubic centimeter/minute to ft^3/h:
15 cubic centimeter/minute = 15 × 0.00211888 ft^3/h = 0.0317832 ft^3/h
