Cómo convertir T1C (carga útil) a SCSI (rápido)

1 SCSI (Fast) = 29.7619047619 T1C (payload)

15 T1C (payload) = 15 × 0.0336 SCSI (Fast) = 0.504 SCSI (Fast)
