Cómo convertir IDE (DMA Modo 1) a Ethernet (gigabit)

1 IDE (modo DMA 1) = 0,1064 ethernet (gigabit)
1 ethernet (gigabit) = 9,3984962406 IDE (modo DMA 1)

Ejemplo: convertir 15 IDE (DMA modo 1) a ethernet (gigabit):
15 IDE (DMA modo 1) = 15 × 0,1064 ethernet (gigabit) = 1,596 ethernet (gigabit)
